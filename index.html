Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is kOnshu?

kOnshu is a modern, curated digital ecosystem and sandbox featuring cutting-edge web applications, creative developer tools, technical resources, and botanical e-commerce solutions. It serves as an experimental sandbox for high-performance frontend and backend engineering.

What projects are currently hosted in the kOnshu ecosystem?

The ecosystem includes four primary projects in active beta development:

  • konshu.xyz: An interactive Art & WebGL particle sandbox experimenting with audio-visual browser APIs.
  • kcode.konshu.in: A technical blog database sharing scalable software tutorials and clean code snippets.
  • plant.konshu.in: A bespoke houseplants e-commerce marketplace integrated with Stripe checkout.
  • tools-cli.konshu.in: A command-line installer hub for zero-dependency local shell developer setups.
Who built kOnshu and is it open source?

kOnshu is designed, engineered, and maintained by Karan, a UI/UX developer and software engineer. Many of the underlying configs, scripts, and WebGL modules are open source and shared on GitHub, with links accessible through the ecosystem pages.

How do I get in touch or track development milestones?

You can follow the real-time timeline on our roadmap, connect with Karan on Twitter/X (@karan_k_code), or check out the developer's professional LinkedIn profile. For support queries, please reach out via social media direct messaging.
